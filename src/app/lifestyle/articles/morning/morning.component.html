A productive morning can set you up for a fantastic day. It's important to maximize those early hours so you stay in control and can roll with any punches that come with the rest of your day. Here, I'll share my typical morning routine.

7:20am
Alarm goes off. Hit snooze.
7:29am
Alarm goes off again. Hit snooze again.
8:02am
Wake up. Realize that instead of hitting snooze that last time, you turned off the alarm entirely, and now you're running late.
8:23am
Look at Reddit on your phone until you're ready to get out of bed.
8:30am
Wash your face. Floss obsessively. Floss again.
8:35am
Realize a pill is missing from your weekly pill case and fumble through your cabinet until you find the original bottle. It's empty. Text your psychiatrist for a refill and brace yourself for a rough day.
8:45am
Look through the fridge. You know you're not going to make breakfast. Or lunch. You eye the turkey slices you know you're not going to finish, but they're not expired enough to throw out yet.
9:03am
Leave for work.
9:04am
You forgot your security badge. Come back.
9:05am
Leave for work. You're very late.
