About

How to Stay Single Forever is a parody dating blog. It's a static site built with Angular. I wanted to challenge myself to build a full site with lots of features and functionality, while keeping it all accessible and using my own designs.

All the icons on the site were created by me. Many of the images were taken from or based on content from undraw.co. Other images are credited where they appear.

There's more information about the technical side of the side on the project's readme, which you can find on Github. I encourage you to explore the site here instead, and see if you can find all the content!

The content on this site is all inspired, in some way, by my own experiences. Dating is hard. I’m fine though. Don’t worry.

Also, because this is based on my own life, it's pretty heteronormative. Sorry about that. I hope you still enjoy it even if you don’t identify with it.
